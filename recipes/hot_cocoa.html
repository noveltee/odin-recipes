Creamy Hot Cocoa

Description

It's old fashioned, it's comforting, it makes the kitchen smell wonderful and it's good for the soul.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/3 cup boiling water
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup half-andhalf cream

Steps

  1. Combine the cocoa, sugar and pinch of salt in a saucepan.
  2. Blend in the boiling water.
  3. Bring this mixture to an easy boil while you stir.
  4. Simmer and stir for about 2 minutes. Watch that it doesn't scorch.
  5. Stir in 3 1/2 cups of milk and heat until very hot, but do not boil!
  6. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
  7. Divide between 4 mugs.
  8. Add the cream to the mugs of cocoa to cool it to drinking temperature.
